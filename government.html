Data Layer

What the data layer captures.

Every tap generates a cross-sector data point. Anonymised and aggregated by district, mall, industry, time, and consumer segment.

Footfall Intelligence

Real-time tap volume by merchant, mall, and district. Peak hour mapping. Day-of-week and seasonal patterns. Repeat visit frequency per consumer segment.

Cross-Sector Spend Flows

How consumers move between F&B, retail, beauty, healthcare, and services within the same district. Cross-industry loyalty patterns nobody else has.

District Retail Health Index

A live merchant tap index per district. Leading economic indicator for Singapore's physical retail sector — updated daily, not quarterly.

Consumer Segment Profiles

Anonymised demographic and behavioural segments built from consent-based tap data. PDPA-compliant. No individual-level data ever shared.

Tourist vs Local Splits

Visitor spending patterns at Changi, Parkway Parade, and East Coast malls. Real-time tourist vs local spend splits by industry and time.

Urban Planning Signals

Retail vitality by postal district. Mall catchment mapping. Cross-district consumer mobility. Data for planning decisions currently relying on manual surveys.
