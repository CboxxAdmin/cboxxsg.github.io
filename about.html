The Team

Built for Singapore's
small businesses.

CBOXX helps physical merchants grow by turning every customer visit into a lasting relationship with one tap, no app, and no complexity.

Why We're Building This

The infrastructure physical retail never had

86.9% of Singapore's retail happens in physical stores. But the entire loyalty and data infrastructure was built for e-commerce. Physical merchants have been left behind.

We asked: what if every merchant in a mall could have the same loyalty and data capabilities as a major e-commerce brand, without the complexity or cost?

The data is the real asset. The loyalty OS is how we earn the right to collect it

Our Vision

A thriving Singapore where small businesses never get left behind

Behind every shop is an owner who wakes up early, stays late, and pours everything into what they built. We want to live in a Singapore where those people win where the auntie running the kopitiam and the guy behind the bubble tea counter have every advantage they need to grow

Our Mission

Help SMEs grow by turning every customer visit into a lasting relationship

Helping SMEs grow is not just what we do it is why we exist. CBOXX gives small merchants the tools to retain customers, understand their business, and compete on their own terms. One tap, real loyalty, real growth

How We Build

What we believe.

01
Simplicity by design

We strip away complexity so merchants can focus on running their business, not managing software.

02
Merchant outcomes first

Every decision we make is measured against one question does this help merchants retain more customers?

03
Partnership over transaction

We are not just a platform. We grow when our merchants grow, and we take that responsibility seriously.

04
Built for Southeast Asia

Designed from the ground up for the dense, high-footfall retail environments that define our region.

05
Responsible data practices

Consumer consent is explicit and transparent. Data is never sold. We earn trust through how we handle information, not just what we promise.

06
Long-term thinking

We are building infrastructure meant to last not a quick loyalty gimmick, but the foundation of physical commerce intelligence in Singapore.
