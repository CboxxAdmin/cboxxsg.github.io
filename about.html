The Team

Built by founders
who move fast.

Five co-founders across commercial, tech, ops, people, and creative. Singapore-based. Building Singapore's first NFC-powered district loyalty network.

Why We're Building This

The infrastructure physical retail never had.

86.9% of Singapore's retail happens in physical stores. But the entire loyalty and data infrastructure was built for e-commerce. Physical merchants have been left behind.

We asked: what if every merchant in a mall could have the same loyalty and data capabilities as a major e-commerce brand, for SGD 100 to start?

The data is the real asset. The loyalty OS is how we earn the right to collect it.

How We Build

What we believe.

⚡
Move fast, learn faster

We'd rather ship something imperfect and learn from real merchants than spend months building in isolation.

🔍
Data over opinions

Every product decision is grounded in what merchants actually need and what consumers actually do.

🤝
Merchant-first

If merchants don't win, we don't win. Every feature and price point runs through that filter.

🌏
Built for Southeast Asia

Starting in Singapore for infrastructure clarity — but this is a regional play from day one.

🔒
Privacy by design

PDPA compliance is a competitive advantage. Consumer consent is explicit. Trust is our moat.

📢
Build in public

We document the journey — the wins, the setbacks, the lessons. Building in public keeps us accountable.
