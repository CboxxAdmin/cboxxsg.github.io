Why we're building CBOXX.

86.9% of Singapore's retail happens in physical stores. But the entire digital loyalty infrastructure — apps, CRMs, data platforms — was built for e-commerce. Physical merchants have been left behind.

We asked a simple question: what if every merchant in a mall could have the same loyalty and data capabilities as a major e-commerce brand, for SGD 100 to start?

The answer is CBOXX. One NFC tap. No app. No friction. Real data. And because we sit across every industry in a mall — F&B, retail, beauty, healthcare, fitness — we see cross-sector consumer behaviour that nobody else can.

That data is the real asset. The loyalty OS is how we earn the right to collect it.

How We Build

What we believe.

⚡
Move fast, learn faster

We'd rather ship something imperfect and learn from real merchants than spend months building in isolation. Speed is our competitive advantage as a small team.

🔍
Data over opinions

Every product decision is grounded in what merchants actually need and what consumers actually do. We build from merchant pain points, not assumptions.

🤝
Merchant-first

The network is only as strong as its merchants. If merchants don't win, we don't win. Every feature, every price point, every decision runs through that filter.

🌏
Built for Southeast Asia

Physical retail dominates Southeast Asia. We're starting in Singapore because of its infrastructure and data regulatory clarity — but this is a regional play from day one.

🔒
Privacy by design

PDPA compliance isn't a checkbox — it's a competitive advantage. Consumer consent is explicit. Data is anonymised. Trust is our moat.

📢
Build in public

We document the journey — the wins, the setbacks, the lessons. Building in public keeps us accountable and attracts the right people to what we're creating.
