Loyalty Models

Choose how you
reward customers.

Three loyalty models included in every plan. Switch anytime.

⭐
Points
Earn points per tap. Redeem for rewards you set. Customer sees their balance grow every visit.

Points loyalty

Set how many points customers earn per tap. Set what they can redeem points for — free item, discount, exclusive offer. You control the rewards.

Best for high-frequency visits where customers come back weekly. Keeps them engaged between visits because they can see their points growing.

Best for: Cafes, bubble tea, nail bars, salons
🎯
Stamp Card
Digital version of the classic stamp card. Tap 8 times, get a reward. Zero learning curve for customers.

Stamp card

The simplest loyalty model. You set how many taps to earn a reward and what the reward is. Customers understand it instantly — no explanation needed.

Replaces every physical stamp card in your customer's wallet with one digital tap. No lost cards, no fake stamps, no hassle.

Best for: F&B, coffee shops, dessert stalls, hawkers
🏆
Tiered
Bronze, Silver, Gold. Customers level up by visit frequency. Your best customers get your best perks.

Tiered membership

Create a VIP experience for your most loyal customers. The more they visit, the higher their tier — and the better their perks.

Customers are motivated to visit more to reach the next tier. Your Gold members become brand advocates who tell others about your shop.

Best for: Retail, beauty, healthcare, fitness studios
