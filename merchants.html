Choose Your Loyalty Model

Three models.
One tap.

Every plan includes all three models. Switch anytime.

⭐
Points

Earn points per tap. Redeem for rewards you set. Customer sees their balance grow every visit.

Points loyalty

Set how many points customers earn per tap and what they can redeem. You control the rewards — free item, discount, exclusive offer.

Best for high-frequency visits where customers come back weekly.

Best for: Cafes, bubble tea, nail bars, salons
🎯
Stamp Card

Digital stamp card. Tap 8 times, get a reward. Zero learning curve for customers.

Stamp card

The simplest loyalty model. Set how many taps earn a reward and what the reward is. Replaces every physical stamp card in your customer's wallet.

No lost cards. No fake stamps. No hassle.

Best for: F&B, coffee, desserts, hawkers
🏆
Tiered

Bronze, Silver, Gold. Customers level up by visit frequency. Your best customers get your best perks.

Tiered membership

Create a VIP experience. The more they visit, the higher their tier and the better their perks. Gold members become brand advocates.

Customers are motivated to visit more to reach the next tier.

Best for: Retail, beauty, healthcare, fitness
