Privacy Policy

CBOXX Pte. Ltd. (UEN: 202613969N) is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

What we collect: When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message. When a consumer taps a CBOXX NFC tag, we collect visit data with your explicit consent.

How we use it: Contact form data is used solely to respond to your enquiry. Consumer tap data is used to power loyalty programs and, with your consent, anonymised and aggregated for business intelligence purposes.

Third-party sharing: We only share anonymised, aggregated data with third parties (brands, mall operators, government agencies). No individual-level data is ever shared without your explicit consent.

Your rights: You may withdraw consent, request access to your data, or request deletion at any time by contacting info@cboxxsg.com.
