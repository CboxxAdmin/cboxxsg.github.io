How It Works

Four steps.
One tap.

01
We install together

We come to your counter and set up a small NFC tag. 30 minutes. Works on every modern smartphone — nothing for your customer to download.

02
Customer taps

One tap at your counter. PDPA-compliant consent, instant enrolment. No friction for your customer — they are in within seconds.

03
You see what's happening

A live dashboard showing tap volume, repeat rate, peak hours, and which customers are at risk of not coming back.

04
Loyalty grows

Campaign tools, segment targeting, cross-sector benchmarking. Data that drives real decisions.
